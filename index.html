How to use AI Recipe Recommendation System

Fill in your food preferences, cooking style, cooking time and excluded ingredients, and the system will recommend a suitable recipe according to your needs.

Dietary preference

Choose your eating habits, such as vegetarian, vegan, low carb, etc., and the system will prioritize recipes that meet these criteria.

Cuisine preference

Choose your favorite style of cooking, such as Chinese, Western, Japanese, etc., to help the system select recipes that suit your tastes.

Cooking time

Set the maximum amount of time you are willing to spend cooking, and the system will recommend recipes that can be completed within this time frame.

Remove ingredients

If you are allergic to or dislike certain ingredients, you can enter them here and the system will avoid recommending recipes that contain them.
